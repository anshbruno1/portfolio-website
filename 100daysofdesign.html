  • In Personal Projects

VIEW IN LANDSCAPE MODE

My Purpose Behind This

I recently got a very important advice from an experienced person that it is important for me to understand and learn design as strongly as a person who is at a Design school. I do not have a design degree but I wish to work in the field of UI/UX and Product Design. I am currently entering my fourth year now and I still have one year left before I get a good job. I wish to improve my skills as a designer so that I can say without a doubt that I can perform as well as a student who has been to design school.

I have decided to spend at least an hour every single day from today 4th April to learn Design online and update my progress here on this blog.







I have collected some sources from YouTube and will try to cover them one by one with time. These are my targets. I wish to cover these in 100 days:

  • ABNUX

    (run by Head of Design at Unacademy, Abhinav Chhikara)

    Link to the YouTube Channel

  • AJ&Smart

    (run by a Product Design and Innovation studio based in Berlin)

    Link to the YouTube Channel

  • The Futur

    (based out of Santa Monica, California - learing the Business of Design)

    Link to the YouTube Channel

  • Medium

    (all kinds of interesting articles on Medium)

    Link to Medium

I will update this page (I'll try :P) every night to share what I learnt along with my work samples so that even I can see how much have I grown in the next 100 Days.

I will upload a video explaining my day's learning because it takes a lot of time to embedd HTML. It would be easier for me if I just record my progress and attach a link. There might be few days where I take off (college and exams) but I will not stop until 100 Days are over.

so here it begins!
