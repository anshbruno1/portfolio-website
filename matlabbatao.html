Matlab Batao

  • 2017 - 2018

I have been a keen student of Hindu Mythology and the lessons our Mythology teaches us. As I grew up, I realised that a lot of my friends were not able to understand the importance of knowing the wisdom our ancient scriptures contained. The biggest reason was that these stories were not presented in a way young audience would connect to. I decided to share my own learnings from Hindu Mythology through short videos through a series called Matlab Batao
