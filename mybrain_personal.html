Personal Projects

I have always got an urge to make designs or projects that weren't meant for a competition or Hack. I just wanted to crystalise my own thoughts by taking up personal projects. There were some times when I wished to learn a particular skill or practice and that is when I made some plans to incorporate those skills in personal initiatives. These ideas were not meant for any Hack or Competition, these were only to work towards my own vision

March 2018

Daimoku Tracking Concept App

I am a follower of Nicherin Buddhism and I have been chanting regularly since childhood. I felt like making an app that could help me keep a track of my Daimoku through a simple, clean and efficient UI. This is just an app concept and not an actual app.

Sept 2018

HCI Design Principles

In my Fall Sem 2018, I took Human Computer Interaction as an elective subject. We were asked to work on a UI and test it against two most popular Design Tests. I collaborated with two of my friends Aritro Paul & Harsh Verma.

December, 2018

Hack/>Off 2019

happy to share that my team and I won the best User Interface Design award at Hack/>Off 2019 powered by GitHub organised by IET VIT. They gave us a GitHub bag and it is almost a dream come true because I had seen this bag before but never thought I could own it.

March 2018

Code2Create 2018

Sponsored by big shots like IBM, GitHub, DigitalOcean and Slack, Code2Create was a great Hackathon. Our team's app Epione secured the First Position and got the Best User Interface / User Experience Award. We won Rs. 27,000 and a lot of cool stickers.
